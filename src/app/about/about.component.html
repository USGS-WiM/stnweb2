Important usage notes and instructions

Many of the supplemental GIS data layers found in the map are from a range of sources and are not maintained by WiM. We offer these map layers as a decision support supplement to the STN sites layer, but we cannot guarantee their performance and availability. Many of these externally maintained layers are large datasets and may load slowly depending on network latency. In some cases they may fail to load entirely when network latency is high.

Many of these outside layers are maintained by NOAA. Click here to subscribe to a NOAA email list for GIS service status updates.
